CHARACTERS

“RE”
Born a hero of the environment, but when the night comes, RE
feels the urge to destroy the Earth due to his strong DNA inherited
from his father, ‘The Dark.’ His trademarks consist of sentimental
eyes and an engravement of the amount of time he has to live on
his inner arm.

“Aurora”
RE’s powerful supporter. She was brought to life by the
nature spirits ‘N’. She dies every time the environment
gets destroyed. Her characteristics include courage and
confidence.

“N”
Naissance, Nature

Fire, Air, Water, Ground, and Tree spirits : They are the guardians of nature who help ‘RE’ find ‘COSMO’. They are the creators of ‘Aurora.’
