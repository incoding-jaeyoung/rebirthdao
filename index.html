ABOUT US

ECO-TAINMENT

Rebirth DAO is a DAO executing global Eco-Entertainment projects that encompass the promotion of underlooked Global ESG activities, making it easy for individuals, corporates, and institutions to participate in saving Earth.

P2E
P2E: Plant to Earn
Reducing Carbon emissions
R2E
R2E: Recycle to Earn
Reducing Environmental pollution

Rebirth DAO’s Eco-tainment contributes to reducing carbon emissions through the ESG project. Rebirth DAO’s NFT embodies values to protect the environment through providing events, rewards, and entertaining communities where users can be the leaders of environmental activities.

ARTWORK

‘Landscape Artwork’ captures the beauty of the Earth. Professional photographers and experienced artists can collaborate
to become the leaders of the ESG activities.

EMBT

EMBT

Project Rebirth DAO operates within the ‘EMBT’ Platform where users can mint NFTs and receive
rewards. In this ecosystem, users can participate in ESG activities such as P2E (Plant to Earn) and
R2E (Recycle to Earn). Take part in this ESG movement to reduce carbon emissions and work towards
NET ZERO by sharing the beauty of nature, planting trees, and using eco-friendly products.
